
E-R Model for relationships
    Diamond: relationship, 
        "Married to",

    Total Participation:
        every entity in a set is in the relationship
        double line connector to the diamond
    
    Partial Participation:
        not all entities are involved
        single line connector to the diamond
    
    Hierarchical Representation:
        Generalization
            separate entity sets
                common characteristics
            example: 
                Dog, cat, hamster,
                    common: pets,
                        owner,
                        name,
                        feed,
        Specialization:
            pet 'is-a'
                dog,
                cat,
                hamster
